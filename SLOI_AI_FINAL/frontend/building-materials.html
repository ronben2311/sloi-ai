The system knows the freight matrix. A Libyan buyer gets Egypt-origin rebar (3 days) not Turkey-origin (5–6 days). A Nigerian buyer gets Algeria-origin aggregate (Atlantic route, 10 days) not Saudi-origin (Red Sea + Suez, 22 days). Landed cost, not just FOB price.

LOI with full spec references
Building material LOIs must reference the right standards — ASTM A615 for rebar, EN 572 for glass, ISO 4422 for PVC pipe. SLOI AI's LOI templates are pre-loaded with the correct spec references for each product category, so the document is bankable from day one.
🛡️
Reconstruction-zone compliance
Libya, Iraq, and Sudan require enhanced KYC and end-use verification. SLOI AI screens all reconstruction-zone deals against OFAC, EU, and UN sanction lists and performs enhanced due diligence before issuing any LOI — protecting suppliers and brokers from compliance exposure.
🔗
HBAR for quality verification
Material quality disputes are costly in construction. Every SLOI AI deal includes an inspection reference (SGS or BV) and the inspection report hash is recorded on Hedera blockchain alongside the LOI hash — giving buyers, sellers, and project owners a tamper-proof quality audit trail.
