Trader Agreement
Please read and confirm before submitting

1. Trader Obligations

You agree to supply verified, genuine products as listed. You will respond to RFQ alerts within 24 hours. You will not contact buyers directly outside of SLOI AI for any deal originated on the platform for a period of 24 months.

2. Non-Circumvention

You agree not to circumvent SLOI AI and deal directly with buyers introduced through the platform. Violation results in immediate termination and a penalty equal to 10% of the circumvented deal value.

3. LOI Fee

You agree to pay the agreed LOI fee per signed LOI. The default rate is $500 USD. This can be renegotiated annually. Fees are invoiced monthly.

4. Supplier Anonymization

You agree that SLOI AI will not disclose your factory names, cities, or contact details to buyers. Products are listed anonymously with only origin country visible.

5. Compliance

You confirm you do not trade with sanctioned entities or countries as defined by OFAC, EU, and UN sanctions lists. You will immediately notify SLOI AI of any compliance concern.

6. Termination

Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice. Pending LOIs remain binding after termination.
