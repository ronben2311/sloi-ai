How SLOI AI works
Three steps · Two are yours · Full audit trail
01
Pick a corridor & set your mandate
Choose a corridor tab above, then filter by theater, region, or origin using the sub-filter pills. Click Contact on any product row to reach the supplier directly, or Deal Desk to open the AI negotiation engine with that product pre-loaded.
You · takes 3 minutes
02
AI Orchestration Engine negotiates
Up to 7 negotiation stages with a KYC-cleared, OFAC-screened counterparty — all within your private price ceiling. Your mandate is never exposed. The engine monitors corridor alerts, risk scores, and live price deltas (▲▼) in real time and stops the moment your ceiling is hit.
Orchestrator · 10–48 h
03
You authorize — full paperwork issues instantly
One click generates 7 legal documents — LOI, NDA, SPA, LC Application (UCP 600), Trade Contract (Incoterms 2020), HS Code & Duty Report, and Freight Advisory — dispatched to all parties. Nothing moves without your authorization.
You · always in control
