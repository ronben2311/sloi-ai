// Common Questions
What is the difference between SaaS, Agentic Team, and Setup?›
SaaS ($99/$299) — the agent runs on SLOI AI's cloud servers. You buy credits and spend them per session. Best for occasional use or getting started.

Agentic Team ($599/mo) — install the SLOI AI MCP skill on your own machines in 2 minutes. Agents run locally in Claude Desktop, Cursor, or Windsurf. Choose the Construction or Advanced Factory procurement profile — each has its own mandate rules. Unlimited sessions, flat monthly fee, data stays on your servers.

Setup ($999 one-time) — full on-premise deployment on your servers. White-label LOIs and NDAs, unlimited users, custom mandate rules, and dedicated 48h onboarding. One payment — the platform is yours forever with no recurring fees.
Can the agent close a deal without my approval?›
No — this is a hard rule across all plans. The Boss must approve every deal before a LOI is generated. The agent negotiates and reaches the best available offer, then stops and waits. The LOI is only generated after your explicit one-click approval. On Enterprise you can configure conditional auto-approval within a pre-set price mandate, but the default is always manual approval.
What happens to my data on the SaaS plan?›
On the SaaS plan, negotiation sessions and mandate data are processed on SLOI AI's cloud infrastructure. Supplier identities are always anonymized — you never see supplier names, only REF codes and origin country. If data residency matters to your team, choose the Agentic Team or Setup plan where all data stays on your own servers.
Do credits expire? Can I get a refund?›
Credits never expire. If you decide SLOI AI isn't right for you, unused credits are fully refundable — just email billing@sloiai.com within 90 days of purchase. The Agentic Team monthly plan cancels with 30 days notice, no penalty. The Setup one-time payment is non-refundable after the onboarding call begins.
Can I upgrade from SaaS to Agentic Team later?›
Yes. SaaS and Agentic Team are separate products — you can start on SaaS credits to test the platform, then switch to the Agentic Team monthly plan when your team is ready. Any unused SaaS credits remain valid. To switch, contact support@sloiai.com and we'll handle the migration.
Does SLOI AI handle payments or SWIFT transfers?›
No. SLOI AI is a negotiation and LOI platform only. Once a LOI is signed, payment flows directly between the buyer and the trader — outside SLOI AI entirely. We don't hold escrow, process SWIFT, or touch commodity payments. This keeps the platform simple and avoids financial licensing requirements.
What AI models work with the Agentic Team plan?›
Any MCP-compatible agent host: Claude Desktop (Anthropic), Cursor, Windsurf, Continue, or any custom framework that supports the MCP protocol. The SLOI AI skill works identically across all of them — add 4 lines of JSON to your config. The underlying model (Claude, GPT-4o, Gemini, etc.) is your choice.
Is a LOI legally binding?›
The LOI generated by SLOI AI is binding upon countersignature by all parties. It follows ICC trade terms and establishes the agreed price, quantity, incoterm, payment terms, and delivery schedule. It is not a final sales contract — the actual purchase contract and payment happen directly between buyer and supplier after the LOI is signed.
