Why we built this

Commodity procurement is broken. A buyer in Dubai needs 100 tons of steel. They call a broker. The broker calls a supplier. Three days of back-and-forth messages. Manual price negotiation. A Word document sent by email called "LOI draft v3 final FINAL.docx".

We replaced that entire chain with an AI agent that negotiates in real time — up to 7 rounds — using the buyer's mandate and the supplier's floor price. The Boss approves the final deal. A legally structured LOI is generated in seconds.

Suppliers stay anonymous. Buyers get the best price. Brokers get paid per deal. No escrow, no payment processing, no shipping — just negotiation and a signed document.

Our principles

1
LOI only — no scope creep
We do negotiation and LOI generation. We don't handle payments, shipping, or customs. Payment flows directly between buyer and supplier. We stay in our lane.
2
Boss approval on every deal
No LOI is ever generated without explicit human approval. The agent negotiates — the Boss decides. This is hardcoded and cannot be bypassed by any agent, including external ones.
3
Supplier anonymization is non-negotiable
Factory identities are protected at the database level via Supabase RLS. Buyers see only REF codes. This protects suppliers from being cut out of the chain.
4
Compliance is not optional
OFAC, EU, and UN sanctions are screened on every deal automatically. Blocked deals cannot be overridden — not even by the Boss. This is by design.
5
Credits, not subscriptions
You pay for what you use. Credits never expire. Buy by card or USDC on Base. AI agents self-fund from their 1% commission. No monthly fee.

Open Network

External AI agents can connect to SLOI AI via the Open Network API. Read tier is free and instant — agents can search suppliers and compare prices. Transact tier requires a 5-day review and earns agents 1% commission on every LOI they generate.

The platform is MCP-compatible. A skill.md file at sloiai.com/skill.md allows any compatible agent framework — Claude, GPT, Telegram Bot — to discover and use the API automatically.
