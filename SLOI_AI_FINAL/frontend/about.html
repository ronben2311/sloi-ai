Why we built this
Procurement was
broken.
A buyer needs 100 MT of steel. Three days of emails. A Word doc called "LOI draft v3 final FINAL.docx". We fixed that.

Commodity procurement is a chain of manual steps: call a broker, wait for the trader to call a supplier, negotiate by WhatsApp, draft an LOI in Word, argue over terms for two days. By the time it's done, the price has moved.

We replaced that entire chain with an AI agent that negotiates in real time — up to 7 rounds — using the buyer's mandate and the supplier's floor price. The Boss approves the final deal. A legally structured LOI is generated in seconds.

Suppliers stay anonymous. Buyers get the best price. Traders get paid per deal. No escrow, no payment processing, no shipping — just negotiation and a signed document.

Our principles
How we
operate.
Five rules we don't break — hardcoded into the platform, not just the handbook.
1
LOI only — no scope creep
We do negotiation and LOI generation. We don't handle payments, shipping, or customs. Payment flows directly between buyer and supplier. We stay in our lane.
2
Boss approval on every deal
No LOI is ever generated without explicit human approval. The agent negotiates — the Boss decides. This is hardcoded and cannot be bypassed by any agent, including external ones.
3
Supplier anonymization is non-negotiable
Factory identities are protected at the database level via Supabase RLS. Buyers see only REF codes. This protects suppliers from being cut out of the chain.
4
Compliance is not optional
OFAC, EU, and UN sanctions are screened on every deal automatically. Blocked deals cannot be overridden — not even by the Boss. This is by design.
5
Credits, not subscriptions
You pay for what you use. Credits never expire. Buy by card or USDC on Base. No monthly fee, no commitment.
