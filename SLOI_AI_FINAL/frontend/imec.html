Why this corridor.
Why now.

The IMEC corridor was announced at the G20 summit in New Delhi in September 2023. It is the most significant trade infrastructure initiative since the Belt and Road. The corridor is designed to move goods — physical commodities — faster and cheaper between Asia, the Middle East, and Europe.

Every kilometre of rail, every port expansion, every logistics hub requires steel, cement, pipes, energy, and agricultural supply. Every one of those purchases needs a negotiated price and a signed agreement between buyer and supplier. That agreement is a Letter of Intent.

The region is awash with capital — Saudi Vision 2030, NEOM, UAE industrial expansion, Iraq reconstruction, Egypt's New Administrative Capital. All of it needs commodities. All of it needs procurement infrastructure. There is no AI-native procurement platform built for this corridor. Until now.
